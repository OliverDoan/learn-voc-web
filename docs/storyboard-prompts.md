# Storyboard prompts — Truyện chêm Oliver & Lisa (Unit 10–15)

> Prompt tạo ảnh **6 khung minh hoạ, KHÔNG nhúng chữ** cho từng truyện.
> App tự hiển thị nội dung truyện bên dưới ảnh, nên ảnh chỉ cần vẽ cảnh.
> Mỗi mục "Từ vựng" chỉ để bạn đối chiếu — KHÔNG cần render vào ảnh.
> Cách dùng: dán **STYLE & CHARACTER SHEET** trước, rồi dán block prompt của unit.

---

## 🎨 STYLE & CHARACTER SHEET (dán đầu mọi prompt)

```
Style: warm anime/manga illustration, soft cel-shading, Studio-Ghibli-like,
clean linework, cozy lighting. ONE image split into 6 equal comic panels
(2 rows x 3 columns). Each panel shows only a small numbered circle (1–6) in the
top-left corner. NO captions, NO speech bubbles, NO text anywhere in the image —
illustration only. Consistent characters across all panels.

Characters (keep faces, hair, outfits identical in every panel):
- Oliver: young Vietnamese man, short dark hair, friendly smile, casual jacket. Nickname "Sunny".
- Lisa: young woman, long brown hair, pink hoodie, cheerful/expressive (a foreigner).
- Roommate: young woman, long dark hair, mustard-yellow sweater (housemate sharing the flat).
```

## Unit 10 — Khu phố mới của Oliver và Lisa

```
Panel 1: Oliver & Lisa at their apartment doorway; a kind elderly neighbor greets and chats warmly with them.
Panel 2: Wide bright view of a modern, freshly-built residential block and neighborhood, green and lively.
Panel 3: A quiet residential street with a clearly visible ONE-WAY traffic sign, slightly winding road.
Panel 4: Building ground floor showing a small CLINIC and a GYM storefront side by side, with their signs.
Panel 5: Cozy warm apartment interior in the evening; Oliver & Lisa relaxing on the sofa, calm peaceful mood.
Panel 6: Balcony view of a children's PLAYGROUND and a blue swimming POOL below, lots of greenery; Oliver smiling.
```
Từ vựng (24): inform, block, develop, safe, vibrant, area, friendly, invite, one-way, clinic, ground, complicated, gym, chilled, noisy, vibe, always, property, comfy, cosy, playground, pool, mostly, everywhere

## Unit 11 — Chuyến du lịch đầu tiên của hai người

```
Panel 1: Oliver & Lisa excitedly planning a trip at home, leaning over a world map and a travel brochure.
Panel 2: At a desk, applying for a visa and booking a hotel online on a laptop, passports nearby.
Panel 3: At the airport, a departure board reads DELAYED; Lisa looks a bit disappointed while Oliver comforts her.

```

## Unit 12 — Một ngày tham quan cùng nhau

```
Panel 1: A friendly local guide leads Oliver & Lisa along a scenic, historic-looking route.
Panel 2: A montage of monuments in one panel: a grand mausoleum, an old church, a temple, a pagoda, a war museum.
Panel 3: A crowded touristy spot on one side; a quiet hidden lovely corner on the other where the couple stands close.
Panel 4: A lively local food market; the guide points at street food while Oliver & Lisa taste dishes.
```

## Unit 15 — Lisa và thời tiết quê chồng
